About Lafayette Homes

We design and build custom homes across the Upstate of South Carolina with a simple promise: clear communication, disciplined budgets, and craftsmanship that lasts.

Our Approach

Transparent budgets and schedule discipline. You’ll know where every dollar goes and what happens each week. We coordinate design, permits, selections, and trades so you don’t have to.

What We Build

Single-family custom homes with practical, high‑value specs: efficient envelopes, durable finishes, and livable floor plans. Side‑garage layouts, bonus rooms, and home offices are our wheelhouse.

Process (6 Steps)

  1. Intro call & site review
  2. Pre‑construction budget & scope
  3. Architecture & selections
  4. Permitting & mobilization
  5. Build & walkthroughs
  6. Closeout & warranty
